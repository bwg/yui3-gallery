revive

Defined in src/gallery-base-revive/js/base-revive.js:77

Event for the revive method, fired prior to revive. Invoking the preventDefault() method on the event object provided to subscribers will prevent revive from occuring.

Subscribers to the "after" moment of this event, will be notified after revive of the object is complete (and therefore cannot prevent revive).
